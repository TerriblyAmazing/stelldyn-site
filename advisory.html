Advisory & Strategy

Turning market complexity into opportunity.

Every business sits inside a unique web of costs, markets, and logistics. We help producers see it clearly — then capture more of the value it holds.

The work

Map the value. Then capture it.

Stelldyn maps value chains, models market economics, and pinpoints exactly where value is created and where it leaks. We combine operational expertise with analytical rigor, so producers understand not just what their materials are worth — but how to keep more of it.

What we deliver

  • Market & pricing analysis — demand dynamics, price formation, competitive position
  • Supply chain mapping — cost-to-serve, routing, and reliability
  • Byproduct & circular strategy — reuse, sale, and recovery potential of byproducts and downgraded material
  • Capability building — systems, dashboards, and playbooks that give your team control
  • Advisory retainers — ongoing partnership for continuous insight
