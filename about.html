About Stelldyn

Advising with integrity. Connecting supply to circular demand.

A boutique consulting and trading partner for the industrial materials sector — packaging, paper, recovered fiber, plastics, metals, timber, chemicals, and the byproducts that flow alongside them.

Who we are

Advice always comes before a transaction.

We help clients manage material flows with transparency, responsibility, and efficiency, bridging global supply with circular demand. Our advisory-first model means every recommendation is aligned to your interest — not a hidden margin. When we do trade or represent you in the market, that same transparency carries through.

Leadership & expertise

Built by operators, not theorists.

Stelldyn was founded by Josh Silcox , a supply chain and commercial executive with 15+ years leading sales, sourcing, category strategy, supplier governance, and logistics across packaging, CPG, and global manufacturing.

Before founding Stelldyn, he led commercial and supply chain innovation at Fortune 500 consumer products and packaging manufacturers — managing a $1B+ direct-materials portfolio, supporting 50+ manufacturing sites and 80+ suppliers, running a business with distribution across 30+ countries. We've sat on your side of the table, which is what separates our advice from theory.

$50M+ In annual enterprise value through market-based sourcing, supplier optimization, trading mechanisms, and pricing intelligence.
$15-30M In annual EBIT improvement via category strategy, product qualification, and TCO-based sourcing decisions.
$100M Global recycling and industrial-packaging business run with full P&L ownership, serving customers in 30+ countries.
~30% OTIF improvement by instituting S&OP discipline across sales, operations, sourcing, inventory, and logistics.
$2.5M In 3PL cost reduction by restructuring Incoterms and logistics execution models.
$1M+ In annual non-conforming-product cost avoided through a cross-functional raw-material qualification process.
