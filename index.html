The challenge

Materials markets are opaque — and value leaks out.

Industrial materials move through fragmented, international channels where pricing is hidden, quality is inconsistent, and intermediaries take margin you never see.

Producers struggle to reach the right buyers. Buyers can't verify what they're getting. Byproducts that should generate revenue end up as cost. Stelldyn sits between producers, manufacturers, recyclers, and brands — telling you where your materials should go, what they're worth, and how to capture more of that value, then helping you execute.

Common questions

What people ask about Stelldyn

What does Stelldyn do? +
Stelldyn is a boutique advisory and trading firm for the industrial materials sector. We help producers, manufacturers, brands, and recyclers buy, sell, and recover materials and byproducts — including paper, recovered fiber, plastics, metals, and chemicals — through advisory, transparent market representation, and digital market connection.
What does "advisory-first" mean? +
Advice always comes before any transaction, and our recommendations are aligned to your interest rather than a hidden margin. When we trade or represent you, the same transparency carries through — no undisclosed markups.
Which materials does Stelldyn cover? +
Pulp, paper, and recovered fiber; packaging and containers; plastics and polymers; metals and scrap; textiles; and agricultural and organic byproducts — plus the logistics that move them.
Who does Stelldyn work with? +
Material producers, global brands, logistics and distribution operators, private-equity firms, and consulting or legal firms needing materials subject-matter expertise.
